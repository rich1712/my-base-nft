# MyNFT Whitepaper

## Abstract
MyNFT is a community driven NFT collection deployed
on Base Mainnet. It is designed to reward early
supporters with presale access, staking rewards,
governance voting and exclusive token gated content.

---

## 1. Introduction
The NFT space has grown rapidly over the past few
years. MyNFT aims to build a sustainable and
community focused NFT project on Base Mainnet
that gives holders real utility and value.

---

## 2. Technology
MyNFT is built on the following technology stack:

- **Blockchain:** Base Mainnet (Ethereum L2)
- **Standard:** ERC721 + ERC2981
- **Language:** Solidity ^0.8.20
- **Library:** OpenZeppelin Contracts
- **Tools:** Remix IDE, Basescan

---

## 3. Tokenomics
- **Total Supply:** 100 NFTs
- **Presale Price:** 0.005 ETH
- **Public Sale Price:** 0.01 ETH
- **Royalty:** 5% on secondary sales
- **Max Per Wallet:** 3 NFTs
- **Max Per Whitelist:** 5 NFTs

---

## 4. Roadmap

### Phase 1 - Launch
- Deploy NFT contract on Base Mainnet
- Launch presale for whitelisted addresses
- Launch public sale for everyone

### Phase 2 - Growth
- Launch airdrop campaign
- Launch staking contract
- Build minting website
- Grow community on Discord

### Phase 3 - Expansion
- Launch governance contract
- Add token gating for holders
- Launch second NFT collection
- Apply for Base ecosystem grants

---

## 5. Community
MyNFT is built for the community. Holders will
have access to exclusive content, governance
voting rights and staking rewards.

---

## 6. Conclusion
MyNFT aims to be a sustainable and community
focused NFT project on Base Mainnet. We believe
in building real utility for our holders and
growing together as a community.
