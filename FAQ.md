# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### What is MyNFT?
MyNFT is a simple ERC721 NFT collection deployed
on Base Mainnet. It features presale, public sale,
whitelist, staking and airdrop support.

### What blockchain is MyNFT on?
MyNFT is deployed on Base Mainnet which is an
Ethereum Layer 2 blockchain built by Coinbase.

### How much does it cost to mint?
- Presale price: 0.005 ETH
- Public sale price: 0.01 ETH
- Whitelisted addresses mint at presale price

### How many NFTs can I mint?
- Regular wallets can mint up to 3 NFTs
- Whitelisted wallets can mint up to 5 NFTs

---

## Minting

### How do I mint an NFT?
1. Connect your wallet to Base Mainnet
2. Go to the minting website
3. Click the mint button
4. Confirm the transaction in your wallet
5. Wait for the transaction to complete

### What wallet can I use?
Any Ethereum compatible wallet works such as
MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet or Rainbow Wallet.

### How do I get on the whitelist?
Follow our community on Discord and Twitter
to get notified about whitelist opportunities.

---

## Staking

### What is staking?
Staking allows you to lock your NFT in a contract
to earn rewards over time.

### How do I stake my NFT?
Call the stake() function with your token ID on
the staking contract after deployment.

### Can I unstake anytime?
Yes you can unstake your NFT at any time by
calling the unstake() function.
